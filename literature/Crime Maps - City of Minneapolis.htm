Crime Maps

Interactive Crime Maps

Minneapolis Police Interactive Map Application 

The Minneapolis Police Department provides an interactive crime map through RAIDS online.

RAIDS Online is a public resource of general crime information. It is best viewed with Internet Explorer 7 or Firefox or Chrome.  The city of Minneapolis does not make any warranty, representation, or guarantee as to the content, sequence, accuracy, timeliness or completeness of any of the database information provided herein for any reason. All aspects of the data provided here are susceptible to a degree of error due to the complexities of the process involved in compiling and programming the data.

Click the link to go to the RAIDS Online Crime Map. NOTE:  You will be taken to an external website.

Historical Weekly Precinct Maps (2009-January 2014)

Precinct maps were produced on a weekly basis. There is a map for violent crimes and one for various other crimes including property crimes and drug offenses. Find your Police Precinct.
